D.O.B. 1950

Studied Fine Art Disciplines at Western Illinois University, and minored in education.

A multidisciplinary contract artist and sculptor of 33 years with very diversified areas of experience in concept design, engineering, fabrication, transportation, and installation of project solutions.

Photos of his work as an artist have been published in Interior Design Magazine, USA Today, and National Geographic. His studio has been producing items and art products for business complexes, museums, zoos, development sites, shopping centers, amusement industry, motion pictures, T.V. commercials, and residential areas.

The only rules of engagement that must be adhered to on any project are common sense, physics, applicable construction codes, and client satisfaction.

His most valuable asset is having the experience to be able to provide multiple solutions for any clients concepts.
